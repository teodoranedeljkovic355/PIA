Three new Laboratories of the Innovation Center of ETF


Innovation Center (ICEF) is founded by University of Belgrade – School of Electrical Engineering (SEE). We collaborate with SEE’s laboratories and research groups, research and teaching staff, and include them in ICEF projects to create the best teams and results. ICEF is founded as LTD, 100% owned by SEE, and registered as Innovation Center with the Serbian Ministry of Education, Science and Technological Development.


Ceremonial awarding of certificates to the participants of IT retraining


Participants in the IT retraining program who attended trainings in Java and php programming languages at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, University of Belgrade, successfully passed the final exam and completed professional internships. On this occasion, a ceremonial awarding of certificates was organized in the amphitheater of the Lola building, which was attended by representatives of the Government and UNDP, who also financed this program.
